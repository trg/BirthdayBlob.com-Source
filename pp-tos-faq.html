When I click Login, nothing happens - I'm aware of this bug, for some reason sometimes my code thinks you're not logged in when you are. Clearing your cookies fixes it. Will investigate soon.

Why don't all my friends show up? - Birthday Blob can only access data that your friends privacy settings allow to be shared, so if a friend does not allow her birthday to be shared, it will not show up on this list.

Privacy Policy

Birthday Blob does NOT store any of the information provided by Facebook. In fact, the entire Facebook interaction is client-side via Javascript, In laymans terms, it means that your privacy is ensured because only your browser is talking to Facebook and none of that information is being sent to me.
